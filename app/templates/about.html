Our Mission

Paws Rescue Center exists to give rescued animals a safe place to heal, grow, and find loving homes. We believe every animal deserves patience, care, and a second chance.

What We Do

  • Rescue animals from unsafe or uncertain situations
  • Provide food, shelter, and medical attention
  • Match pets with responsible adoptive families
  • Support adopters with guidance after adoption
